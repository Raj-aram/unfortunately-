Frequently asked questions about LexConnect.

Log in as a Client → click Find Advocate in the navbar → filter by Court Level or Location → open an advocate's profile → click Book Appointment and fill in the date, time slot, and meeting mode.

The Pay Fees button appears on your My Appointments page only after the advocate accepts your booking. Payment is simulated — no real money is charged in this prototype.

Once an appointment is Accepted and the mode is Online, a Chat button appears. Both the client and the advocate can open the chat room and exchange messages in real time. The chat polls the server every 3 seconds automatically.

After registering as an Advocate, you are redirected to the Profile Setup page. Fill in your courts of practice (Supreme / High / District), city, consultation fees, bio, and available time slots (comma-separated, e.g. 10:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 5:00 PM).

Currently, once an appointment is Accepted or Rejected, its status cannot be changed. In a production version, a cancellation workflow with notifications would be added.
